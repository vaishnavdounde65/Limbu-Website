About Limbu

At Limbu, we are passionate about bringing you the ultimate refreshing experience with our signature limbu soda. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a drink that captures the essence of summer in every sip. Using only the finest lemons and a touch of fizz, we have perfected a recipe that is both delicious and invigorating. Our commitment to quality and taste has made us a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Join us in celebrating the joy of refreshment, one glass at a time.
